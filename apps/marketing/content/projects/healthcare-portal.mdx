## Challenge

The BrainStrong Foundation needed a modern website to support their annual brain health awareness campaign. Their existing site was difficult to update, had poor mobile performance, and wasn't converting visitors into screening participants.

## Our Solution

We built a content-managed site with a clear campaign narrative, interactive risk assessment tools, and a streamlined sign-up flow for free community screenings. The CMS integration allowed the foundation's small team to publish articles and update event information without developer support.

## Results

The new site drove a 3x increase in screening sign-ups during their annual campaign. Mobile traffic engagement improved by 80%, and the foundation's team now publishes weekly content independently, keeping the site active between campaigns.
