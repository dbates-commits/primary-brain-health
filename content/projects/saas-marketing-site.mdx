## Challenge

NeuroWell Clinic wanted to expand their dementia prevention services beyond their geographic area but lacked the digital infrastructure to deliver high-quality virtual care. They needed a platform that felt professional, was easy for older patients to navigate, and met strict telehealth compliance requirements.

## Our Solution

We designed a patient-facing platform with a streamlined booking flow, secure video consultations, and integrated cognitive assessment tools. The interface was tested extensively with users aged 55+ to ensure clarity and ease of use across all devices.

## Results

Within six months, NeuroWell expanded their patient base by 65% and now serves patients across three states. Patient satisfaction scores for the virtual experience consistently rate above 4.7 out of 5.
