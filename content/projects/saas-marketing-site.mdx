## The Challenge

CloudFlow needed a marketing website that could keep pace with their rapidly evolving product. Their previous site required developer involvement for every content change, slowing down their go-to-market efforts.

## Our Solution

We built a fully content-managed site using Tina CMS with block-based editing. The marketing team can now create landing pages, update pricing, and launch campaigns without any code changes.

## Results

Time-to-publish for new content dropped from days to minutes. The marketing team launched 15 new landing pages in the first month, contributing to a 3x increase in demo bookings.
