## Challenge

The National Cognitive Health Institute needed a reliable, user-friendly digital platform for administering validated cognitive screenings across multiple clinical trial sites. Existing tools were outdated, difficult for participants to use remotely, and lacked the data infrastructure needed for large-scale research.

## Our Approach

We built a secure, HIPAA-compliant web application that standardizes cognitive screening administration while making the experience intuitive for participants aged 50 and older. The platform supports both in-clinic and remote assessment modes with real-time data capture.

## Results

The platform has been used across 12 clinical trial sites with over 2,000 participants. Remote completion rates exceeded 90%, and researchers reported a significant reduction in data entry errors compared to the previous paper-based workflow.
