## The Challenge

RetailMax was struggling with high cart abandonment rates and a dated user interface that didn't perform well on mobile devices. They needed a complete overhaul that would modernize their brand while improving key metrics.

## Our Approach

We conducted extensive user research to identify pain points in the existing checkout flow. The new design prioritizes mobile-first experiences, reduces steps to purchase, and implements real-time inventory updates.

## Results

Within three months of launch, RetailMax saw a 40% increase in conversion rates, 60% improvement in mobile engagement, and a significant reduction in customer support tickets related to checkout issues.
