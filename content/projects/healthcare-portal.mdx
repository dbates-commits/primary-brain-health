## The Challenge

MedCare needed to modernize their patient experience while maintaining strict HIPAA compliance. Their legacy portal was difficult to use and didn't support modern features patients expected.

## Our Solution

We created a modern, accessible portal with real-time appointment scheduling, secure messaging, and telehealth video calls. The content management layer allows staff to update FAQs and resources without IT involvement.

## Results

Patient portal adoption increased by 200%. Phone call volume to the clinic dropped 40% as patients could self-serve for common tasks. Patient satisfaction scores improved from 3.2 to 4.7 out of 5.
