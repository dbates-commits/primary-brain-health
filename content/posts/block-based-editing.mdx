Block-based editing has revolutionized how we think about content management. Instead of rigid templates, editors can compose pages from pre-built, styled components.

## Benefits for Content Teams

Marketing teams love block-based editing because it gives them creative freedom without requiring technical knowledge. They can drag, drop, and rearrange sections to tell their story effectively.

## Benefits for Developers

Developers maintain control over the design system. Each block is a React component with defined props, ensuring brand consistency and code quality regardless of how editors combine them.

## The Variant Pattern

Our showcase demonstrates the variant pattern - each block supports multiple visual layouts. A Hero block might be centered, split, or gradient-based. Same content structure, different presentations.
