Good content modeling is the foundation of a maintainable CMS implementation. Let's explore patterns that scale with your content needs.

## Referenced Content

Instead of duplicating author information across posts, we reference a single Author document. Update once, reflect everywhere. This is essential for testimonials, team members, and any reusable content.

## Global Content Blocks

Some content appears across multiple pages - CTAs, promotional banners, announcement bars. Create these as global blocks that can be referenced anywhere, ensuring consistency and easy updates.

## Nested Objects vs Collections

Know when to use nested objects (FAQ items within a page) versus separate collections (blog posts). Nested works for page-specific content; collections work for content that lives independently or gets referenced.
