After completing your cognitive assessment with Primary Brain Health, you'll receive a detailed report. Understanding what these results mean is the first step toward building an effective brain health strategy.

## What We Measure

Our assessments evaluate multiple cognitive domains including memory, attention, processing speed, executive function, and language ability. We use validated clinical instruments that allow us to compare your performance against age-adjusted norms and track changes over time.

## Reading Your Risk Profile

Your risk profile takes into account your cognitive screening results alongside other factors: family history, cardiovascular health, sleep quality, physical activity levels, and more. This comprehensive view helps us identify which areas offer the greatest opportunity for intervention.

## From Results to Action

The most important part of your assessment isn't the score — it's what we do with it. Your care team uses your results to build a personalized prevention plan targeting your specific risk factors. This might include lifestyle modifications, referrals for further evaluation, cognitive training recommendations, or a combination of approaches tailored to your situation.
