    items:
      - question: Is Primary Brain Health medical care or wellness?
        answer: >-
          Primary Brain Health is a clinically grounded brain health service focused on early
          detection, risk assessment, and personalized next steps. We are
          designed to work alongside your existing healthcare providers. While
          some people come to Primary Brain Health proactively, the service is structured around
          cognitive health assessment and consultation rather than general
          wellness alone.
      - question: Do I need symptoms?
        answer: >-
          No. Many of our patients come to Primary Brain Health because they want to understand
          their baseline brain health or reduce long-term risk — not because
          something feels wrong. Primary Brain Health is designed for people who want to act
          early, not just those already concerned.
      - question: What do I get?
        answer: >-
          A structured brain health assessment, a clear explanation of your
          results, and a personalized plan with next steps. If you choose to
          continue, you get ongoing coordination and support from your Brain
          Health Navigator.
      - question: What happens after?
        answer: >-
          After your assessment and consultation, you receive a written plan
          tailored to your biology, risks, and goals. You can move forward
          independently, with navigator guidance, or with a family member
          looped in.
      - question: Can I use my HSA or FSA to pay for Primary Brain Health?
        answer: >-
          In most cases, yes. Primary Brain Health provides the documentation you need to submit
          to your HSA or FSA administrator. Reimbursement depends on your plan
          — check with your administrator if you have questions.
      - question: Is it covered by insurance?
        answer: >-
          Primary Brain Health is not currently billed through insurance. We are a
          direct-to-patient service, which allows us to spend more time on your
          brain health rather than on coverage constraints. HSA/FSA funds are
          typically eligible.
      - question: Who reviews my results?
        answer: >-
          Your results are reviewed by our clinical team, including a
          board-certified neurologist, and shared with you in a structured
          consultation. A Brain Health Navigator helps you understand the
          results and next steps.
      - question: Is meeting with a Brain Health Navigator required?
        answer: >-
          It's optional but recommended. Most people find the navigator
          conversation is where the assessment becomes personally useful —
          translating results into a plan you can act on.
      - question: What documentation will I receive for HSA/FSA submission?
        answer: >-
          You will receive an itemized receipt with the clinical service
          described in terms your HSA/FSA administrator can evaluate. If your
          administrator requires additional documentation, we can help provide
          it.
